    <h4><strong>My Research</strong></h4>
</center>

<p style="margin-left: 1.5cm"> </p>
My research focuses on:

- **Dexterous Manipulation** integrating model-free learning with physics-based simulation, optimization, and control for solving contact-rich dexterous manipulation efficiently and reliably.

- **Robot Learning from Human** design structured learning methods to enable robots learn skills from human interactions,
with safety, robustness, and data efficiency.

<p style="color: red; font-weight: bold;">I am actively looking for summer/fall 2026 internship opportunities in robotics and AI.</p>
